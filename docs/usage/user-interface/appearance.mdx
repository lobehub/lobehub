LobeHub offers a wide range of keyboard shortcuts, allowing you to perform various actions quickly and efficiently. Mastering these shortcuts can significantly reduce mouse usage and streamline your workflow.

## Accessing Settings

Click on your user avatar in the top-right corner of the homepage. From the dropdown menu, select "App Settings," then click on "Shortcuts" to view the available keyboard shortcuts.
